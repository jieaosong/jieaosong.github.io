Jieao Song

I am currently a Postdoc researcher at University of Milan, working in the group of Chiara Camere.

I got my PhD in July 2022 at IMJ-PRG, Paris Cité University, under the supervision of Olivier Debarre.

I was previously a BMS Dirichlet Postdoc researcher at Humboldt-Universiät zu Berlin, in the group of Gavril Farkas.

My field of research is complex algebraic geometry. More specifically, I am interested in the geometry of K3 surfaces and hyperkähler manifolds. More can be found on my Research page.
